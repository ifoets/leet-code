💡 Interview Tip (VERY IMPORTANT)
For 10+ years, interviewers expect:
•	Trade-offs ❌ not definitions
•	Failures ❌ not happy paths
•	Production stories ❌ not theory
A strong answer sounds like:
“We hit backpressure when upstream Kafka spikes exceeded consumer capacity, so we switched from Observable to Flowable with bounded buffering and applied retry with jitter.”
